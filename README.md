# SP-500
## Overview
Thi project analyzes S&P 500 companies and index performance using Power BI and SQL Server. The goal is to transform raw financial and market data into interactive dashboards that provide insights into the company performance, market capitalization, and profitability.
## Objectives
The project aims to answer the following business questions:
-Which companies have the highest market capitalization?
-Which companies generates the highest Earnings Before Income, Tax, Depriciation and Amortization (EBITDA)
?-How has the S&P 500 index changed over time?
-What are the monthly ad daily trends in index performance?

## Key Insights
-Large-cap Companies dominate the S&P 500 in terms of market value
-EBITDA varies significantly across companies, highlighting differences in profitability
-The S&P 500 index demonstrates long-term growth with short-term fluctuations
